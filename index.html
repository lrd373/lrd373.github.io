About Me

I've always loved how code transforms abstract ideas into beautiful, functional projects — it always feels like magic. From my work in natural language processing at Columbia University, to specializing in Microsoft SSRS reports at the UK's leading cloud SaaS company for the arts, Spektrix, I have relished building a client's vision with code. So, when COVID-19 upended the performing arts sector and sent the whole world into virtual workplaces, I immediately saw the critical role that web technologies have to play. I expanded my programming skillset with some of the most popular languages and frameworks in modern web development — React, Bootstrap, Node.js, and more. With these tools, I strive to build applications that are accessible, responsive, and always engaging.
